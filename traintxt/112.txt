Abstract： Ginseng has a long history of medicinal use in China and is a
traditional and valuable Chinese medicinal material.
Its main active
ingredient， ginsenoside - Rg3 （ G - Rg3） ， has received much attention
because of its extensive pharmacological effects.
As a natural plant
medicine，
G - Rg3 plays an important role in the prevention and
treatment of
tumors
by
inducing
cell
apoptosis，
inhibiting
tumor
proliferation
／
metastasis，
improving
immunity
and
reversing
drug
resistance. Based on the literatures， this paper summarized and analyzed
the anti - tumor effect and mechanism of G - Rg3 to promote the further
development and utilization of G - Rg3.
